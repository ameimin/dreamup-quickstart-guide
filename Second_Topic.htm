Dreamup.ai Introduction

Dreamup is an AI art application. It is free and can be used by anyone.

Using the App

Once logged into your account, you are ready to start creating.

  1. Go to the Create tab at the top menu.
  2. Select the model type. The appropriate model tokens will appear, depending on your selection.
  3. Type in your prompt. To get the best results, be as detailed as possible.
  4. Add an image from a website or upload one from your computer.
  5. Click the Submit button.
